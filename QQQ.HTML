Biography

I am a graduate student in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Texas at Dallas graduating in May 2021. I have a Bachelor of Technology degree from the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Guwahati.

Previously, I worked at Works Applications, Singapore as a Software Engineer, where I worked on delivering central libraries, microservices and distributed multi-tenant architecture to thousands of developers inside the company who utilized them to build various products for our customers. Aside from work, I am also an open-source enthusiast and have created projects that were listed in GitHub trending.

My interests lie in solving problems related to large-scale distributed software systems.
